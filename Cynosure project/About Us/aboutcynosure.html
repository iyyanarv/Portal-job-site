About Us

Cynosure.com is the most innovative and second largest online job portal in India. Founded in 2008, over the past decade, Cynosure.com has become a prominent name in the recruitment industry. The popularity of the portal is evident from the fact that it has crossed the 3.4 crore candidate landmark and has more than 3 lakh latest job vacancies from leading companies on the site.

Cynosure.com connects jobseekers and recruiters by accurately matching candidate profiles to the relevant job openings through an advanced 2-way matching technology. While most job portals only focus on getting candidates the next job, Cynosure focuses on the entire career growth of candidates. To this end, Cynosure has launched Cynosure Learning- India’s largest career skills site for working professionals with over 500+ courses & certifications.

As the industry shifts towards mobile, Cynosure.com is leading the transition and is the fastest growing job portal on mobile devices, witnessing a 100% YOY growth in mobile traffic and also offers on-the-go jobs through the Cynosure Job Search App.

Cynosure works closely to bridge the gap between talent & opportunities and offers end-to-end recruitment solutions. Cynosure Job Fair brings candidates and top employers under one roof. While Cynosure HR Conclave brings top HR leaders to share insights on latest trends, innovations & best practices in the HR industry. Cynosure also has a large reach through its print product, Cynosure Jobs – the Tuesday Job supplement of Hindustan Times– making it the only job portal with an integrated print and online offering.
